Alphabet Mod

This is Alphabet Mod v1.0 . You can use it to make big labels and signs in Manic Digger worlds. It uses block IDs 402 through 431 and includes blocks representing letters "A" through "Z", symbols "!." and "space". Feel free to edit the Alphabet Mod to your liking. I have more mods in the pipeline so stay tuned.

Expanded Blocks Mod

Have you been thinking that the default Manic Digger blocks are a little bare-bones? Well then this mod is for you. It includes a total of 58 blocks! It features two new types of glass, two types of fences with a fence gate, trapdoors, a computer and TV, 4 switchable lights, and a host of other things. Clear glass (one of the new glass types) has snow on it in the winter season. In survival mode you can now get pumpkin seeds (occasionally) from breaking dirt and grass. You can use the seeds to grow pumpkins which you can craft into jack-o-lanterns and pizza (rather unrealistic). This mod also includes land mines that, although small, can kill you instantly. You can also craft many of these items. Important Note: Crafting does not seem to be working for me, so it is possible that there is a problem with the craftability of some items.
